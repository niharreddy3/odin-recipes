Lasagna

Description

Delicious classic lasagna. Noodles, cheese & meat.

Ingredients

  • Beef Chunk
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Cook beef chunk until brown
  3. Combine meat, onion, garlic & tomatoe sauce in pot
  4. Heat the mixture until it reduces
  5. layer noodles, sauce, & cheese
